Tickets

Get your tickets for the most anticipated classical music event of 2025. Early bird tickets are now available with special pricing for students and groups.

Choose from our different ticket options including single day passes, weekend packages, and VIP experiences with exclusive access to rehearsals and meet-and-greets with the artists.

All tickets include access to the main performances, program booklets, and complimentary refreshments during intermissions.

Agenda

Our carefully curated program spans across multiple days, featuring works from the Baroque period to contemporary compositions.

Day 1: Opening night featuring Beethoven's Symphony No. 9

Day 2: Chamber music performances highlighting string quartets

Day 3: Piano recitals and concertos

Day 4: Grand finale with a full orchestral performance

Artists

We are proud to present a lineup of world-renowned musicians and emerging talents from across the globe.

Our festival features solo performers, chamber ensembles, and full orchestras, bringing together diverse interpretations and musical perspectives.

Special guest conductors will lead our festival orchestra in performances of classical masterpieces and exciting new commissions.

Location

The festival takes place in multiple iconic venues across the city, each chosen for its exceptional acoustics and historical significance.

Main concerts will be held in the Grand Concert Hall, with additional performances in the Historic Opera House and the Modern Arts Center.

All venues are centrally located and easily accessible by public transportation. Accommodations are available nearby, with special rates for festival attendees.
